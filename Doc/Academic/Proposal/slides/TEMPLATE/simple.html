Why RAD?

A methodology emphasizing rapid prototyping and frequent user feedback over rigid planning.

It condenses analysis, design, and testing into short, iterative cycles, ensuring functional components (like ZKP Auth) are delivered and tested early.

PHASES: Requirements • User Design • Construction • Cutover
Early Visualization
Prototypes allow early verification of ZKP & AI logic, reducing the risk of requirement misinterpretation.
Time Constraints
Essential for the strict FYP timeline. Reusable components allow delivery within the academic semester.
Flexibility
Testing results (e.g., AI accuracy) can immediately influence design changes without halting the schedule.
Risk Mitigation
Modular development isolates technical risks. Issues in ZKP won't break the Chat module.
