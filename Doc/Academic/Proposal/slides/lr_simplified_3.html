Domain 03

AI Anomaly Detection

Literature Review
Approach Literature Findings Comparative Verdict
Method A:
Standard ML		 Older algorithms like SVM tend to "underfit" the data. They miss complex/novel attacks due to the noisy nature of real-world data. Natha (2022) REJECTED
Lacks the sophistication to catch modern, subtle anomalies.
Method B:
Hybrid Cloud Models		 Accurate for URL scanning, but requires sending sensitive data to the cloud. This introduces latency (slowness) and privacy risks. Karim et al. (2023) REJECTED
Unacceptable latency and privacy exposure for real-time messaging.
Method C:
On-Device Edge AI		 Using "Tiny" models on-device achieves 96% accuracy (Gaurav) with <100ms latency (Bispo), matching cloud performance locally. Bispo et al. (2025); Gaurav et al. (2025) SELECTED (SentriZK)
Provides high accuracy with zero latency and absolute privacy.
